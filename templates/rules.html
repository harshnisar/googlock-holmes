Event Structure

Introduction

    If you are searching for an event to unleash the puzzle busting instincts in you then you are at the right place. Here you dawn the light of Sherlock Holmes in you, with the help of illustrious aide Mr. Google and fire away the silver-bullets from the tricky guns.

Structure

  • There Will be 2 phases
  • First phase, which will be held on 25th February. The timing for the first phase is 7:30pm to 9:30pm.
  • There will be 20 teams from DA-IICT to be selected and 20 from other colleges for the 2nd phase.
  • The date for 2nd phase is 3rd March and 2nd phase will start at 2:00pm.
  • There are 40 questions, and you are allowed to use google as much as you want.
  • You have a total of 5 passes, which you can use to skip questions you can't find answer to.
  • You cannot return to the skipped question.
  • Each question gives you 10 points. The team with the highest points wins at the ending of the game period. If multiple teams finish with the same score, the team which finished earliest wins.
  • The link to the game will be updated soon. Keep checking and stay tuned.
  • Note:Team from DA-IICT will have to play the 2nd phase from the lab.

Rules

  • Max. team size is 2.
  • Each participant should register only once specifying their teamates..
  • Always answer your question with no spaces(if any) in between words. Don't use punctuations. (i.e. if the answer is “googlock holmes ” write “googlockholmes”)
  • Always answer your question in lower case letters.
  • Once you skip a questions you can't return to that later.
  • For each correct answer your score increases by 10 or by corresponding number if mentioned explicitly.
  • Questions which attract negative marks will be mentioned explicitly.
  • Any sort of malpractice like multiple accounts shall result in disqualification and coordinators decision shall be deemed final.
  • Teams can login in to the specified webpage any time during the event.
  • In case of any disputes the decision of the Team GooglockHolmes would be final.
